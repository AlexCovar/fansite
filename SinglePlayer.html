Even though Smash is a multiplayer game there are still parts that a solo player can enjoy. Challenges, Arcade mode, and Events all come in to keep a solo player entertained for a bit. These game modes get harder and harder as you go on making you need to learn more about the game and keeping you enticed to play.

Special Orders are challenges that "master hand" or "crazy hand" give you. These challenges get more and more difficult as you go on

Classic mode has you choose a difficulty and has you choose your path, you choose who you fight (with the selction given) until you get to the boss, master hand.

Event mode is a bunch of increasingly diffcult challenges that make you choose a certain character or stage, you have to fight your opponent under certain conditions.
